Current Projects

Examination Portal

These include an Examination Division Portal under which the corresponding faculties can submit their internal examination Question Papers which can be downloaded by the admin using the links provided.

Another interesting feature that we are going to implement is that of Automatic Debarred List Generation. The concerned faculties will be provided with a list of students highlighting the classes attended by them before the sessional which will be uploaded by the faculties. Then the Admin according to a predefined criteria will input a percentage and all the students failing to fulfill this percentage will be separated out.

By logging through which they can access or view their details as sessionals internal marks and current attendance , syllabus for internals and study materials related to their curriculum.

Other future projects will be included as per the requirements and smooth functioning of our college.
